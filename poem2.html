Prepare



     Young boy do you remember the stars?
     Flittering fast across the night sky
     Do you remember the young dreams?
     The heartaches?
     The tears and laughs
     Where did the flash of time go?

     Prepare young man
     Fill up your character and height
     Fill your boots and baggy uniform
        Commence this day on the life you wish to lead
     The dreams, the struggle

     Well worn the path will become,
     But lose not hope or joy
     Find the warmth of solace in the
     Heavenly hands that reach down from
     The Sky
     They hold, they strengthen

     March to the cadence of Truth
     Wield it as a double edged blade
     Follow the divine banner descended
     To the stairway to the stars
