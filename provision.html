The Pause in Heaven

       young energetic voice
       stumbles over the words
       tongue twisted
       slowly speak the same
       childish message yearning
       from centuries
       departs at last with humble
       breath
       Amen

The pointed wait
stuck between
devotion and corporate-family worship
the sound poised in
our throats, readied
held back by the dams of our tongues
slowly the behemoth grumbles

Our Father Who
art in heaven
hallowed be thy name...
